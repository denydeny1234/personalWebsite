Prototype

Secondly, I will create some wireframes that will show a simple structure of the website you want. After we agree on it, I will build the prototype that will show you exactly how the website is going to look and behave like.

Develop

Thirdly, after all the modifications have been made, I will begin developing it and create your final product.
