Description

Lockdowns and other limitations made people feel more alienated and made many once-common behaviors seem inappropriate. For some people, this led to the emergence of social anxiety. Since online education was still being offered and social events weren't yet planned, it was much more difficult for international students like the majority of us to socialize and find friends who shared our interests. With connectYall, you can link with people based on shared interests without feeling under pressure to always look presentable or engage in small talk that won't get you anywhere.
